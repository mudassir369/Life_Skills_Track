# Focus Management

## 1. What is Deep Work?
Deep Work means working with full focus and zero distractions. It is the ability to concentrate on one meaningful task for a long time so you can learn faster and produce high-quality work.

## 2. According to the author, how to do deep work properly?
- Remove all distractions
- Set fixed time blocks for focused work
- Work in a quiet, clean space
- Have a clear plan for what you will work on
- Take breaks to rest your mind
- Practice deep work regularly like a habit
