# Learning Proces

## 1. What is the Feynman Technique? Explain in 1 line.

The Feynman Technique is a learning method where you explain a concept in simple terms as if teaching it to someone with no background knowledge.

## 2. What was the most interesting story or idea from Barbara Oakley's TED talk?

**Your brain has two different modes of thinking** - focused mode (active learning) and diffused mode (passive learning). Both are essential for deep understanding and creativity

- Relaxed, free-flow thinking.

## 4. What are the steps to take when approaching a new topic?
- Break the topic into small parts.
- Learn the basics well enough to correct your own mistakes.
- Remove distractions to practice properly.
- Focus on the most important concepts first.
- Practice deliberately, not mindlessly.

---

## 5. What are some actions you can take to improve your learning process?
- Break the topic into small, easy parts.
- Remove distractions and stay focused while studying.
- Take short breaks to refresh your mind.
- Explain what you learned in simple words to check understanding.
- Review your progress regularly and ask for help when needed.
