# Energy Management

## 1. What activities help you relax (Calm quadrant)?
- Listening to music
- Taking a walk
- Sitting quietly or resting
- Watching something light
- Spending time alone to recharge

## 2. When do you find yourself in the Stress quadrant?
- When I have too much work
- When I am learning something difficult
- When deadlines are close
- When things don’t go as planned

## 3. How do you know you are in the Excitement quadrant?
- I feel motivated and energetic
- I look forward to the task
- I feel confident and fully engaged

## 4. Paraphrase “Sleep is Your Superpower”
- Sleep improves memory
- Sleep helps the brain recover
- Lack of sleep harms health
- Good sleep boosts mood
- Sleep strengthens the immune system

## 5. What can you do to sleep better?
- Keep a fixed sleep schedule
- Avoid screens before bed
- Keep the room dark and quiet
- Avoid heavy food late at night
- Relax before sleeping

## 6. Paraphrase “Brain Changing Benefits of Exercise”
- Exercise improves mood
- It boosts focus and attention
- It increases learning and memory
- It reduces stress and anxiety
- It supports long-term brain health

## 7. What steps can you take to exercise more?
- Start with short daily workouts
- Take walks regularly
- Set small fitness goals
- Stretch or move during breaks
